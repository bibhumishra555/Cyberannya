Introduction

Blockchain technology is revolutionizing the way we handle data and transactions in various industries. This debate session aims to explore the various aspects, benefits, and challenges of blockchain technology, fostering a deeper understanding and encouraging innovative thinking among participants. This workshop is chargable for this the pannel decided 30 INR / person

Objectives

  • Eliminating the need for a central authority by distributing the ledger across multiple nodes.
  • Ensuring all transactions are visible to all participants, enhancing trust and accountability.
  • Utilizing cryptographic techniques to secure data and transactions.
  • Making data tampering virtually impossible once it is recorded on the blockchain.
  • Streamlining processes and reducing costs by automating transactions and reducing intermediaries.

Activity Rules

  • Each team will have 5 minutes to present their arguments.
  • Participants must adhere to the allotted time limits.
