Introduction

Welcome to the PPT Presentation session at CyberAnya 3.0. This session aims to provide a platform for participants to showcase their research, innovative ideas, and projects through engaging PowerPoint presentations. This workshop is non-chargable, the pannel decided 0 INR / person.

Objectives

  • Encourage participants to present their ideas clearly and effectively.
  • Foster creativity and innovation in the field of cybersecurity.
  • Provide a platform for knowledge sharing and collaboration.

Activity Rules

  • Each presentation should be no longer than 10 minutes.
  • Participants must use PowerPoint or equivalent software for their presentations.
  • All presentations should be submitted for review at least 48 hours before the session.
  • Participants should be prepared to answer questions from the judges and audience.

Judging Criteria

  • Content Relevance and Quality.[30%]
  • Clarity and Organization.[20%]
  • Visual Appeal.[20%]
  • Content Delivery and Engagement.[20%]
  • Q&A Handling.[10%]

Registration Details

To register for the PPT Presentation session, please complete the online registration form available below on this page. Ensure you provide all necessary details and submit your presentation by the specified deadline.

Recognitions & Rewards

Participants will receive certificates of participation. Winners will be awarded trophies and recognition during the closing ceremony. Additionally, outstanding presentations may be awarded with gift vouchers.

Terms & Conditions

  • Participants must be registered attendees of CyberAnya 3.0.
  • All participants must adhere to the rules and guidelines provided.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  • Presentations must be original work and free from plagiarism.
  • Participants agree to allow CyberAnya 3.0 to use their presentations for promotional purposes.
