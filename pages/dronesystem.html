Introduction

The debate session will focus on the emerging technology of drone delivery systems. Participants will explore the various aspects, benefits, and challenges associated with the use of drones for delivery.This workshop is chargable for this the pannel decided 30 INR / person.

Objectives

  • To understand the technological advancements in drone delivery systems.
  • To discuss the potential benefits and drawbacks of implementing drone delivery.
  • To evaluate the impact of drone delivery on logistics, safety, and privacy.
  • To foster critical thinking and public speaking skills among participants.

Debate Rules

  • Each team will have 1 members.
  • Each team will have 5 minutes to present their arguments.
  • Participants must adhere to the allotted time limits.
  • Respectful and professional behavior is expected at all times.
  • Use of offensive language or personal attacks will lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria

  • Clarity and coherence of arguments.[25%]
  • Depth of knowledge and understanding of the topic.[20%]
  • Ability to rebut the opponent's arguments.[20%]
  • Public speaking and presentation skills.[25%]
  • Teamwork and coordination.[10%]

Registration Details

To register for the Drone Delivery System debate session, please complete the online registration form available below on this page. Ensure you provide all necessary details.

Recognitions & Awards

  • Winners of the debate session will receive certificates of achievement.
  • All participants will receive certificates of participation.
