Introduction

The Module Presentation is a key event in Cyberanya 3.0, where participants will showcase their technical skills and innovative thinking through the development and presentation of a module. This event aims to foster creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving among participants by challenging them to design and implement a solution that addresses a specific problem or improves an existing system.This workshop is free, the pannel decided 0 INR / person.

Objectives

  • Encourage students to apply theoretical concepts in software development, system design, or related technical fields to real-world scenarios.
  • Challenge participants to identify and solve practical problems by developing functional and innovative modules
  • Foster teamwork and collaboration by encouraging students to work together in designing and implementing their projects.
  • Promote creative thinking and innovation in designing solutions that can improve existing technologies or introduce new approaches.
  • Develop participants' ability to present their work clearly and effectively, demonstrating their thought process, technical decisions, and the impact of their solutions.

Activity Rules

  • All modules must be original and developed specifically for this event. Any form of plagiarism will result in disqualification.
  • Each team will have 6 minutes to present their module, followed by up-to 3-minute Q&A session with the judges.
  • Participants must follow the guidelines provided at the beginning of the challenge.

Judging Criteria

  • Problem-Solving Approach. [30%]
  • Technical Implementation. [25%]
  • Innovation & Creativity. [20%]
  • User Experience & Interface. [15%]
  • Presentation & Communication. [10%]

Registration Details

Registration for the Module presentation is free and open exclusively to all registered participant. Please complete the online registration form available below on this page. Ensure you provide all necessary details.

Recognitions & Awards
