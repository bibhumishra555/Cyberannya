Introduction

Welcome to the Virtual Reality Debate Session. This session aims to explore the various aspects and implications of virtual reality technology through an engaging and thought-provoking debate.This workshop is non-chargable.The panel decided 0 INR / person

Objectives

  • To understand the advancements and applications of virtual reality.
  • To discuss the ethical, social, and technological implications of virtual reality.
  • To foster critical thinking and articulate presentation of ideas.

Activity Rules

  • Each team will have 2 members.
  • Each team will have 5 minutes to present their arguments.
  • Participants must adhere to the allotted time limits.
  • Respectful and professional behavior is expected at all times.
  • Use of offensive language or personal attacks will lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria

  • Clarity of arguments and evidence.[25%]
  • Presentation skills and articulation.[20%]
  • Ability to rebut the opponent's points.[20%]
  • Relevance and depth of content.[25%]
  • Overall impact and persuasiveness.[10%]

Registration Details

To register for the 3D printing debate session, please complete the online registration form available below on this page. Ensure you provide all necessary details.

Recognitions & Awards

  • Winners of the debate session will receive certificates of achievement.
  • All participants will receive certificates of participation.

Terms & Conditions

  • Participants must be registered attendees of CyberAnya 3.0.
  • By participating, you agree to abide by the rules and decisions of the judges.
  • The organizers reserve the right to make changes to the event schedule if necessary.
  • All decisions made by the judges are final and binding.
