About CyberAnya 3.O

CyberAnya is an yearly technical fest.

Which is being organized for the third time by the BCA department of LND College, Motihari.

  • Where

    Seminar Hall L.N.D. college, Motihari

  • When

    sep - 17 - 2024 from 10:00 AM

CyberAnya 2024 serves as more than just a stage for showcasing technical skills; it is a pivotal step towards becoming a job-ready programmer. Participants will engage with cutting-edge topics and innovative practices, gaining insights that bridge the gap between academic learning and professional application. Set in the state-of-the-art Seminar Hall of LND College.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find answers to common queries about CyberAnya, registration & participation. Discover how to get started, being a participant of CyberAnya 3.O, and explore our pricing plans. Dive in and get all the details you need to make the most of our offerings!

  • What is CyberAnya 3.0?

    CyberAnya 3.0 is a technical event designed to provide participants with a comprehensive platform for learning, networking, and skill development. Hosted annually, CyberAnya 3.0 brings together students, professionals, and enthusiasts from across the cybersecurity landscape to engage in expert talks, hands-on workshops. With a focus on fostering collaboration, innovation, and community engagement, CyberAnya 3.0 aims to empower individuals to excel in the dynamic and ever-evolving field of cybersecurity.

  • Who can attend CyberAnya 3.0?

    The event is open to students, professionals, and enthusiasts interested in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and related fields.

  • Is there a fee to attend CyberAnya 3.0?

    Yes, there is a registration fee and participation fee also. Detailed information about the fee structure is available on our website ( REG. GUIDELINES ).

  • What should I bring to the event?

    Bring your Coupon ID.

  • Is there a specific dress code for the event?

    Although there is no mandatory dress code, we suggest wearing formal attire. Any specific dress requirements for certain events will be indicated in the event details.

  • What are some of the advantages or benefits that attendees can expect from participating in CyberAnya?

    Participating in CyberAnya offers attendees a myriad of advantages. Firstly, it provides a platform for expanding knowledge through expert talks and workshops, enabling individuals to stay abreast of the latest trends and techniques in the field. Additionally, the event fosters invaluable networking opportunities with industry professionals, peers, and potential mentors, facilitating the establishment of meaningful connections crucial for career growth. Moreover, hands-on participation in workshops and competitions aids in skill development, enhancing practical expertise in areas such as penetration testing and malware analysis. Furthermore, attendees receive certificates of participation and achievement, validating their engagement and potentially bolstering their credentials for future career endeavors. Overall, CyberAnya serves as a catalyst for personal and professional growth within the cybersecurity domain.
